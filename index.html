The NGC Global Service Portfolio

Comprehensive lifecycle support from installation to end-of-life modernization

Service 1/4

EQUIPMENT SETUP

Expert installation and commissioning ensure a long working life of your equipment

Your benefits at a glance

  • • Highly flexible service and excellent cost-benefit ratio thanks to worldwide network of specially trained personnel
  • • Extensive product know-how & operational training of your personnel
  • • Smooth operation of your equipment from day one

Our NGC Global services

INSTALLATION: Expert installation by NGC Global specialists for smooth operation of your equipment. Our installation service includes gearbox setup, fastening on the base plate and mechanical linkage with neighboring drive train components.

COMMISSIONING: Our NGC Global specialists ensure ideal commissioning of your gearbox to start the lifecycle efficiently. This covers installation check, entire drive train alignment, connection to power line, oil filling and test run.

Service 2/4

NORMAL OPERATIONS

Scheduled & preventive maintenance intervals ensure optimal plant performance

Your benefits at a glance

  • • Scheduled maintenance downtimes instead of unplanned outages
  • • Optimized vessel and equipment performance
  • • Planning of inspection intervals suited to operating conditions

Our NGC Global services

INSPECTION: Visual and endoscopic inspections of internals ensure identification and assessment of actual equipment conditions.

PREVENTIVE MAINTENANCE: Regular maintenance measures based on operating hours help keep equipment in optimal condition.

CONDITION MONITORING: Continuous monitoring systems allow condition-based maintenance of your marine transmission equipment.

Service 3/4

INCREASED WEAR

Quick and suitable fault correction services to resolve unplanned equipment downtimes

Your benefits at a glance

  • • Spare parts of original manufacturer quality
  • • Increased productivity thanks to fast repair works
  • • Prevention of vessel stoppages by replacement of critical parts

Our NGC Global services

SPARE PARTS & REPAIR PACKAGES: Individually assembled spare parts packages ensure defective components can be replaced quickly.

GENERAL OVERHAUL & REPAIR: Restore functionalities through repair or replacement of damaged parts or assembly groups.

CORRECTIVE MAINTENANCE: Emergency service to resolve unplanned equipment downtimes as quickly as possible.

Service 4/4

END OF LIFECYCLE

Retrofit and modernization services to increase equipment availability and optimize investment costs

Your benefits at a glance

  • • Optimized investment costs
  • • Increased equipment availability and vessel uptime
  • • Retrofit and modernization for both NGC Global and third party gearboxes

Our NGC Global services

RETROFIT OF GEARBOX: Retrofit existing systems with state-of-the-art technology from our current product range.

REPLACEMENT: One-to-one replacement or spare gearbox solutions to minimize unplanned downtimes.

PARAMETER MODIFICATION: Customized solutions for greater efficiency when existing gearbox needs modification for new working conditions.
